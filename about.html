Our Story

Passion, Tradition, and Culinary Excellence.

A Legacy of Flavors

Founded in 2010, Savor began as a humble family dream to bring authentic, soul-warming Indian cuisine to the heart of the city. We believe that dining isn't just about food; it's an immersive experience that brings people together.

Over the years, our dedication to sourcing the finest organic ingredients and maintaining age-old family recipes has established Savor as a staple gem. Our menu is carefully curated, balancing classic tradition with a touch of modern gastronomy.

At Savor, every spice is ground fresh daily, and every dish is prepared with the kind of love and attention you'd only find in a home kitchen. Welcome to our table.

Meet The Mastermind

Chef Arjun Kapoor

Executive Head Chef

With over 20 years of experience across luxury hotels and Michelin-starred kitchens in Europe and Asia, Chef Arjun Kapoor returned to his roots to create Savor. His philosophy is simple: let the ingredients speak. By combining his deep knowledge of French culinary techniques with the robust, complex spices of his heritage, he elevates classic Indian cuisine into a modern art form.
